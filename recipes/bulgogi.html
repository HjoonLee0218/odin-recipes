Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi, a beloved classic of Korean cuisine, is a dish that embodies a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Thin slices of tender marinated beef are the star, showcasing a perfect balance between sweet and savory notes. The beef is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, garlic, and other seasonings, imparting a rich and complex taste. Traditionally grilled or pan-cooked, the result is succulent and caramelized meat with a slightly charred exterior. The dish is often accompanied by an assortment of fresh vegetables and condiments, allowing for a customizable and dynamic dining experience. With its cultural significance and mouthwatering profile, bulgogi stands as a testament to the artistry of Korean culinary traditions.

Ingredients

Main
  • 300g beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 stalk of green onion
  • 1/3 carrots
Sauce
  • 5 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 Tbsp rice wine
  • 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • Some black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the sauce ingredients and marinate the beef with it.
  2. Prepare the vegetables.
  3. Lightly oil a pan and cook the meat for a little on high heat.
  4. Add the vegetables and cook until on medium-high heat.
